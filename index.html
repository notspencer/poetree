To be a father

I am going to be a father.
What qualifies me as such?
Was I born with the instincts or were they learned?
Am I still learning?
Of course I am. Every day.
Every day, a little closer to meeting this being for the first time.
A little closer to meeting a new part of myself.
Not that this little one is a part of myself, but rather.
My perspective shifts a little, from boy, son, man...
To father, caretaker, provider.
Confidant, encourager, co-head of household.
To lead those who choose you is one thing.
But to lead those who have no choice,
This will take something new. This will take a father.
But not like the one that came before, not exactly.
I will be a lover of life, of laughter, of light.
The encourager, through highs and lows.
When I am low, remembering to support high.
To lift, uplift, and in patience, recognize that I, though a father I may be.
Am not perfect, but learning to build this life now for not only myself,
But to give this new life a warm nook to thrive.
To be a father.

A love era

From my first day of school to today, what a ride.
And all of that time, having you by my side.
A mother so fierce, so passionate and strong.
Chaotic at times, but one rarely wrong.

You've opened your doors, your heard, and your love.
In difficult times, you've lifted me up.
In Auggen, at home, whenever it's tough.
In thunderstorms and life, we've risen above.

Like a bull on the charge, nothing stops when you start,
Energetic and firm, but a tigress at heart!
Cunning and sharp, perseverance and drive,
I've learned from the best, and it's helped me thrive.

Wild child & wild mother, what a wonderful pair.
In good and bad moments, you've always been there.
The highs and the lows, it's felt like a drug.
But really it's been, a Kaltwasser kind of love.

Holiday Stuffing

Greetings today and wunsches galore,
This year's been so great, with so much in store.
From new life so small, and new memories shared,
And at every turn, its so clear that you've cared.

Your powerful love, and wisdom so grand,
We're humbled and grateful to be part of your plans.
Your visits and trips, your time and your wits,
It's nothing to sniff at, for us it's been bliss.
This time of year, hearts and stomachs are filled,
The lights are put up, and warm rooms filled with cheer.
Presents are shared and the smiles, they drag on,
On the faces of all, filled with warmth like the sun.

We still remain grateful, we thank you so dear,
For all that you do, we appreciate you near.
A little more time, some sun, and some shade,
We look forward to these moments and this time of year.
