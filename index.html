To be a father

I am going to be a father.
What qualifies me as such?
Was I born with the instincts or were they learned?
Am I still learning?
Of course I am. Every day.
Every day, a little closer to meeting this being for the first time.
A little closer to meeting a new part of myself.
Not that this little one is a part of myself, but rather.
My perspective shifts a little, from boy, son, man...
To father, caretaker, provider.
Confidant, encourager, co-head of household.
To lead those who choose you is one thing.
But to lead those who have no choice,
This will take something new. This will take a father.
But not like the one that came before, not exactly.
I will be a lover of life, of laughter, of light.
The encourager, through highs and lows.
When I am low, remembering to support high.
To lift, uplift, and in patience, recognize that I, though a father I may be.
Am not perfect, but learning to build this life now for not only myself,
But to give this new life a warm nook to thrive.
To be a father.

May 25, 2023 - St. Pierre Halles, Montmarte, Paris
